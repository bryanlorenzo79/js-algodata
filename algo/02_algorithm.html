What is algorithm?

An algorithm is a set of well-defined instructions to solve a particular problem.

Recipe analogy

Ingredients -> Recipe -> Tasty Dish
Ingredients:

  • cup of noodles
  • seasoning
  • water
Recipe:
  1. Boil water
  2. Pour water into cup
  3. Add seasoning
  4. Close lid and wait 4 minutes

Why algorithms?

As a developer, you're going to come across problems that you need to solve.
Learning algorithms translates to learning different techniques to effeciently
solve those problems.
